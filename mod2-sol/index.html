Generation of Love Live

μ's

μ's was a school idol group formed and led by Honoka Kosaka in Love Live!. They were the school idol group of Otonokizaka High School. The group consisted of nine members and was initially formed with the purpose of bringing attention to the school as it was to be shut down due to a lack of future enrolling students. The group disbanded in the anime after the 3rd years graduated, staying true to their status as school idols.

Aqours

Aqours is a school idol group that was originally formed and led by Kanan Matsuura two years prior to being succeeded by Chika Takami in Love Live! Sunshine!!. They were the school idol group of Uranohoshi Girls' High School. The original group consisted of three members (Third years). The reformed group consisted of nine members along with all original members.

Nijigasaki

Nijigasaki High School also known as Nijigasaki Academy is a high school located in Japan. This is the school that the nine main members of the Nijigasaki High School Idol Club go to.
